I'm a Human-Centered Computing PhD student at Georgia Tech. I work with Alex Endert as a member of the Visual Analytics Lab.

I design and develop visual analytics tools to help people understand complex data, mitigate biases, and make decisions. My current focus is helping NLP researchers and engineers analyze and interpret what large language models (LLMs) have learned during training, how they perform during inference, and how different models compare. I've also worked alongside scientists in field and mission operations to create visual analytics solutions for supporting deep ocean expeditions and scientist-guided spacecraft autonomy.

I have collaborated with incredible designers, engineers, scientists, and researchers at Vanderbilt, Georgia State, Tufts, Emory, Caltech, ArtCenter, Monterey Bay Aquarium Research Institute (MBARI), and NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL).
