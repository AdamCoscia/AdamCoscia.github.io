Hey, I'm Adam! I'm a Human-Centered Computing PhD student at Georgia Tech. I work with Alex Endert as a member of the Visual Analytics Lab.

My research combines Visual Analytics (VA) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) by empirically studying how domain experts analyze data, to build interactive interfaces that visualize the seafloor and address cognitive bias.

I have worked with incredible designers, engineers, and scientists at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory and Caltech.
