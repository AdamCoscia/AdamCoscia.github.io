Hey there! I'm Adam, a first-year Human-Centered Computing PhD student at Georgia Tech, advised by Alex Endert.

I research how on-the-fly data augmentation can improve visual analytics workflows and build awesome prototypes to make these systems real.

I'm also collaborating on developing novel methods for addressing cognitive bias in visual analytics systems.
