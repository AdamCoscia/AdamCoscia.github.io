Hey, I'm Adam! I work at the intersection of Visual Analytics (VA) and Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) developing interactive systems that help people process information to make decisions with data.

My current work investigates how human-in-the-loop techniques can support data discovery and integration while maintaining users' sensemaking capabilities. I'm also collaborating on developing novel methods for addressing cognitive bias in visual analytics systems.

I'm currently a Human-Centered Computing PhD student at Georgia Tech. I work with Alex Endert as a member of the Visual Analytics Lab.

I have worked with incredible designers, engineers, and scientists at NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory and Caltech.
