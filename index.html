Research Interests

I currently work with other Va.lab researchers appling cognitive theory to the design of mixed-initiative VA systems with the goal of raising awareness of and mitigating cognitive biases during task completion. I hope to extend this work into the design and development of user interfaces that make the use of computational methods more human-centered.
