My research sits at the intersection of visual analytics (VA), human-computer interaction (HCI), and artificial intelligence (AI). I build new visual analytics interfaces powered by AI, studying how machine intelligence empowers users to uncover deeper insights in complex data and make smarter, data-driven decisions.

My current focus is on leveraging and evaluating large language models (LLMs) in complex analysis workflows. Combining user-centered design with interactive visualizations, I study how visual analytics interfaces can not only make LLMs more transparent, effective, and usable, but also enhance workflow efficiency and deepen understanding of complex patterns. My research bridges cutting-edge AI with practical, real-world applications, revealing new design opportunities and guidelines at the intersection of AI, data visualization, and HCI.
